Help alphabet This shows the Talon phonetic alphabet.

The Talon phonetic alphabet was designed to contain short words that are easy to pronounce, and distinct words so that none of them, and in particular, no pair of them sound alike.

These words make it so that when you are using the phonetic alphabet to spell things, there is a high degree of accuracy.
